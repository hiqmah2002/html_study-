ABOUT THE NIGERIAN MEATPIE

The Nigerian Meatpie is one of the the most popular and decilious pastries filled with a lot of minced meat (it could be cow meat,ram meat or any meat of your choice preferely cow meat),potatoes(could be sweet or irish but i love the sweet ones.) and carrots. The pastry is always rich in flavour and are slightely flaky which makes them so decilious , I love them so much. They are so popukar in Nigeria and West Africa at whole.You can make them at the comformt of your home because they are easy to make and take only few minutes to be done.

HOW TO MAKE THE NIGERIAN MEAT PIE

We are going to first list our tools and ingredrients needed for our meatpie production

>
Tools Needed
Oven,
Bowls for mixing,
Pans
Sachet paper(optional)
Ingredrients Measurements
Sunflower oil: To cook the filling. 2 Tbsp spoon
Minced beef : Use fresh minced beef to make the filling. 500g of Minced beef
Onion: Add diced onions to add more flavor to the filling. 1 small size onion
Chicken stock seasoning cubes: This is used to season the beef. 2 seasoning cubes or less
Water broth(optional)
Pepper(as Desired)
Thyme: Adds a herby flavor to the filling. 1 tsp of thyme
Potatoes diced: This is a delicious addition to the pie and adds a soft texture to the filling. 2 small size of potatoes
Carrots diced: Add a delcious sweet flavor to the pie. 1 big carrot cleaned and cubed(about a 1/2 cups)
Flour and water mixture: This mixture is used to prevent the meat filling from drying wile the pies bake in the oven. 4 tsp to 6 tsps
Plain flour: Use plain flour to make the dough. Do not use self raising flour as this includes some raising agents. 700g of plain flour
Baking powder : Will be added to the plain flour to make the dough rise 1 teaspoon baking powder
Salt:This will add some flavor to the dough. 1 teaspoon Salt
Margarine or unsalted butte: Use cold margarine or unsalted butter to make the dough. 200g cold Butter, cubed
Cold water: Use cold water to make the dough. 1/4 cup (60ml) cold water
Eggs:Egg wash is used to seal the pie and give it it’s notorious golden color. 2 Eggs
Sugar(Opotional):It is to sweeteen the dough 1 tablespoon sugar

    DIRECTIONS

  1. Make the pastry: in a large bowl mix together flour, sugar, salt and baking powder. Add cubed butter and rub it with the flour until you get a crumbly texture.
  2. Beat 2 eggs with cold water and add it to the flour mixture, mix until dough is formed. Do not over mix! Wrap the dough with cling film and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  3. Make the filling: in a large pan heat oil. Add onion and sauté for 5-6 minutes, until translucent. breaking it apart, for 4-5 minutes, until no longer pink. Season with salt, pepper, thyme and flour.stir and cook for 2 minutes more.
    4. >
  4. Add in cubed potatoes stir well. Add diced carrots and cook for 3-4 minutes.Add the meat and cook, Pour in the water/broth. Stir.
  5. Bring to a simmer over medium-high heat. Reduce the heat to low and simmer for 15 minutes, until thickens. Set aside to cool.
  6. Preheat oven to 350F (175C).
  7. Divide the dough into 2-4 pieces (you also can roll the whole dough, dividing it just making it easier to work). Roll out the dough into 1/8-inch (1/4 cm) thickness. Cut out a round shape with your desired sized. Combine all the leftover dough and roll it again to make more circles.
  8. Place about 1-2 tablespoons of the meat mixture in the center, brush the edges with water and fold over the dough.>Seal it with your fingers or with a fork.
  9. Poke the pies with a fork to create a steam outlet while baking brush the pies with beaten egg.
  10. Bake for 25-30 minutes, until golden. Cool slightly before serving.
